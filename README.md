Healthcare Analytics: Diabetes Risk Stratification using SQL, PostgreSQL and Power BI
Project Overview

This project simulates a real-world Healthcare Analytics initiative focused on identifying diabetes risk factors and developing a population risk stratification framework.

The objective is to demonstrate how modern analytics tools can support healthcare organizations, insurance companies, and population health management teams in identifying high-risk patients and improving decision-making processes.

Business Problem

Diabetes is one of the most prevalent chronic diseases worldwide and generates significant healthcare costs due to complications, hospitalizations, and long-term treatment requirements.

Healthcare organizations need analytical tools capable of:

Identifying high-risk patients.
Prioritizing preventive interventions.
Monitoring population health indicators.
Supporting evidence-based decision-making.

This project addresses these challenges through a complete Business Intelligence workflow.
